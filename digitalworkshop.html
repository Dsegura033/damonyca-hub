About Me

Hi, I’m Damonyca. I’m a self-taught web designer and digital creator with a background in Applied Technology, focused on helping small businesses build a stronger and more intentional online presence.

I specialize in clean, modern websites and simple digital systems that help businesses feel more organized, professional, and easy to navigate online. I also support social media content and branding so everything feels consistent across platforms.

My approach is simple: keep things clear, functional, and visually appealing without overcomplicating the process. I believe every small business deserves a website that actually works for them, not against them.

The Digital Workshop is where I bring all of that together! Combining design, structure, and strategy to help ideas turn into real, usable websites.
